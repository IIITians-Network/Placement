PLACEMENTS


The IIITs were set up mainly to nurture the IT skills to meet the need of growing technology sector. These institute offers undergraduate, graduate and PHD program in IT, electronics and communication and many more. As these institutions are new most of them don't have permanent campus.
Placements are an incredible myriad of options for skilled. Every year, an exceptional percentage of students get placed from many IIIT's. The salary ranges from mediocre to high depending on many traits of an individual's proficiency over a particular stream.
